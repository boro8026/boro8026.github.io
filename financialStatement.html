Financial Statement Analysis

Besides my work in Computer Science, I also took the opportunity to take an entry level accounting course during my senior year. A common topic that is covered during my capstone course and is very applicable to the real world is requirements gathering. Within my accounting course, this was the approach that I took. I was hoping to gain an understanding of how the finances of a company are kept and managed, so that I could potentially aid companies in the future that make their workflow more efficient.

This was a comprehensive final project where I took the publicly available finances of NIKE inc. and analyzed them in a variety of ways in Excel. As great as Excel is, in my interactions, many of the calculations could simply be automated to save time and money in real-life applications. On this project, I received top marks as well as extra credit presenting the final project at an earlier date than most of the class.
