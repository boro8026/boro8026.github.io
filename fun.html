Growing Up Playing Soccer

By far, the biggest aspect of my life was playing soccer from the age of 5 all the way through high school. Playing on a competitive traveling team for many of those years led me to sacraficing over half the days of the week to play. However, it has created my longest lasting friends. Some of which I am still very close with until this day (even though school and life has driven us physically apart). Although my playing days are over, I still have a borderline obsession with the sport. Almost every weekend I get up early just to watch games happening over in the Premier League (England).
