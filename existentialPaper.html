Existentialism

Besides my work in Computer Science, during my college career I also took a liking to Philosophy as well. There was an english class being offered that tackled the philosophy of existentialism, and it was one of the most impactful writing classes I have ever taken.

This was a paper that was written throughout the entire course, and we built on it as we finished books and explored new ideas surrounding existentialism.
